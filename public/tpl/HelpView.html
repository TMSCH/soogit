How does it work ?

Start by searching a song you like in the search field on the top of the page.

Select the video you want to watch : the playlist will be automatically generated, finding other videos similar to the one you chose. The engine look up LastFM database to have the right songs to play after yours.

If you don't want to interrupt the playing video while selecting another one, click on the top left plus sign of one result : the video will start after the one playing ends.
